About Us

MISSION

In the frenetic symphony of our modern lives, it's easy to lose ourselves in the relentless crescendo of tasks, obligations, and distractions. We become instruments playing an unfamiliar tune, the melody of our own lives fading into the background noise. It's in these moments that platforms like Three Friends offer a sanctuary, a sacred space to pause, reflect, and rediscover the harmony within.

VISION

Three Friends isn't just another social media platform; it's a movement, a philosophy woven into the very fabric of its being. It champions the notion that true value lies not in the frantic pursuit of external validation, but in the quiet cultivation of inner peace and meaningful connections. At its core, it emphasizes three pillars: companioning, retreats, and friendships.

Services

Spiritual Direction

Spiritual direction is a practice in which an individual seeks guidance and support from a spiritual director or guide to explore and deepen their relationship with the divine, the sacred, or their own spiritual path. This process is often rooted in a particular religious or spiritual tradition, but it can also be more broadly focused on personal and existential questions.

Retreat Facilitation

Retreat facilitation involves guiding and supporting individuals or groups during retreats, which are dedicated periods of time for reflection, relaxation, learning, and personal or spiritual growth. A retreat facilitator helps create and maintain a conducive environment for participants to step away from their usual routines, connect with themselves, and engage in meaningful activities. This practice is common in various contexts, including religious, spiritual, corporate, educational, or wellness settings.
